Core delivery areas

Broadcast systems built as projects, not only as products.

These solution cards reflect the core UAE business model: factory work, integration, installation and long-term technical ownership for both mobile and fixed broadcast environments.

Mobile production

New OB van builds

Design and build premium OB vans, support trucks and specialist broadcast vehicles from a UAE base with factory-managed integration.

  • Concept design, coachbuilding, rack build, wiring, power, HVAC and technical commissioning.
  • Suitable for sports, entertainment, news and government event production requirements.
Upgrade & refurbishment

OB van refurbishment

Modernize aging vehicles with new routing, replay, audio, intercom, multiviewers, power systems, monitoring and IP readiness.

  • Ideal for extending asset life while improving reliability and operator workflow.
  • Can include phased replacement, documentation updates and post-upgrade support.
Facility integration

Studios, PCR & MCR

Build complete broadcast facilities including studios, production control rooms, MCR, audio rooms, edit suites and technical core infrastructure.

  • Supports greenfield projects, expansions, refreshes and migration from SDI to hybrid or IP workflows.
  • Delivered as integrated scopes covering mechanical, electrical and broadcast systems.
Supporting technology stack

Playout, cloud and transmission supporting layers.

The UAE integration model can be combined with playout, hybrid cloud workflows and the established Thomson transmission product families to create a full end-to-end solution.

Playout systems

Playout & channel origination

Design primary and backup playout, ingest, branding, scheduling interfaces, disaster recovery and central monitoring for single or multi-channel operations.

  • Can be deployed on-prem, in private infrastructure or as hybrid service models.
  • Works well with MCR, archive, OTT and continuity workflows.
Cloud & hybrid workflows

Cloud-based media solutions

Add remote production, cloud playout, recovery workflows, distributed operations and scalable processing for modern broadcasters.

  • Ideal for broadcasters wanting resilience, elasticity and geographically separated operations.
  • Can be packaged with consultancy, migration planning and phased deployment services.
Transmission backbone

Thomson transmission products

Thomson TV and radio transmission products remain part of the wider offer for customers who need complete end-to-end broadcast ecosystems.

  • Supports DTT, radio and national network modernization projects.
  • Complements studio, MCR, playout and cloud scopes with proven RF heritage.
